About Us

Welcome to Quality Control Kennels! We specialize in breeding XL American Bullies with a focus on quality and temperament. Our passion for dogs drives us to produce the best companions for families and individuals alike.

At Quality Control Kennels, we ensure that each puppy is raised in a loving environment and receives the best care. We believe in responsible breeding practices and are committed to the health and well-being of our dogs.
